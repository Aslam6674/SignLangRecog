What is ISL?

Indian Sign Language is a complete natural language with its own grammar and vocabulary — distinct from ASL, BSL, or any spoken Indian language.

📊

18M+ Users

Over 18 million deaf and hard-of-hearing people in India use ISL as their primary language, with regional dialects across states.

🏛️

Official Body

ISL is standardised through ISLRTC — Indian Sign Language Research & Training Centre, under the Ministry of Social Justice.

🤖

How This Works

MediaPipe Hands finds 21 landmarks on your hand. A classifier maps those coordinates to ISL signs — everything runs locally, nothing is uploaded.
